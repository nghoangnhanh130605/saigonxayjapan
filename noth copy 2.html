SGX JAPAN started as an electrical maintenance service company in 2016 under the leadership of KOYAMA and INOUE. Both have over 40 years of experience working in urban facilities and the built environment

The predecessor of SGX JAPAN was the department in charge of security and electrical installation work for SAIGON XAY, a general construction company in Vietnam where Koyama serves as chairman. Inoue started I-TECHNO, an electrical security company in Hanoi in 2017

Koyama and Inoue decided to improve the quality of electrical safety in Vietnam in 2016 and expanded the strategic plan for electrical safety business

Currently, in Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi, we have established cooperative relationships with 2 excellent local electrical equipment companies, 15 highly trained Vietnamese engineers and 3 supervisors
