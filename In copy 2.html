PROCESS FOR OBTAINING THE IDEAL FACTORY OPERATION

In order to manage factory equipment and machines safely and economically, it should be necessary to have high maintenance skills, extensive knowledge of experience, safe and accurate operation and judgment.

Preliminary investigation

It is a simple factory inspection similar to a human health check. We grasp the condition of the factory by measurement of the electrical system and mechanical equipment in no charge. We will give you the survey results in a report. If a detailed inspection is required, we will report it including the investigation budget

Detailed investigation and improvement plan

For issues that are worth improvement, such as ensuring safety, saving energy, and optimizing plant operations, we propose a rational plan that takes into account the economics that match the investment cost. However, for dangerous hardware problems, we will propose separate measures according to the degree of urgency

Improvement proposal and repair work

Based on the improvement plan according to the budget plan decided in consultation with the factory side, product selection and construction budget will be decided. We will conclude a contract based on a fixed budget proposal, but if the order of construction work is a competitive bid, the cost of improvement proposal & design and construction must be divided

Periodic inspection and maintenance work

We recommend to check regularly at the factory according to the Japanese standards. Because Vietnam'law requires high-voltage power supply, fire fighting, and purification equipment by supplier, so there is no inspection of factory equipment. We recommend to make periodic inspection work in low-voltage power distribution system, the machine system for re-evaluate and change oil and replace components according to the manufacturer's standards

Respond and recover to accident immediately

Whenever there is a contract for regular inspection of the factory, whenever there is a problem, our team of engineers will quickly go to the scene to investigate the accident situation and implement the most appropriate measures to restart the factory. After emergency measures, we will conduct a detailed investigation to identify the cause of the accident and propose a solution
